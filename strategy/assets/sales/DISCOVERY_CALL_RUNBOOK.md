# Discovery Call Runbook

## Objective

Confirm fit, urgency, and decision path in 30 minutes.

## Agenda

1. Context (5 min)
- Current protocol stage, launch date, and architecture.

2. Risk map (10 min)
- Highest technical and release risks.
- Current proof/testing/security coverage.

3. Decision path (10 min)
- Budget owner, technical owner, legal owner.
- Buying timeline and blockers.

4. Next step (5 min)
- Agree on scope workshop or proposal date.

## Qualification Questions

1. What is your target launch window?
2. What external reviewers must be satisfied?
3. Which components are most likely to break under stress?
4. What does "good" look like for your release evidence?
5. Who signs the contract and who signs off technically?

## Exit Criteria

1. Clear business problem and urgency.
2. Named decision makers.
3. Explicit next meeting date.
4. Notes logged into CRM within 24 hours.
