# Follow-Up Sequence

## Day 0: Post-Call Recap

1. Send recap with agreed pain points.
2. Confirm next meeting date.

## Day 2: Technical Value

1. Share a relevant proof/test/security example artifact.
2. Tie it to their stated risk.

## Day 5: Commercial Framing

1. Send draft scope ranges (small/medium/large).
2. Ask for preferred path.

## Day 8: Stakeholder Alignment

1. Request inclusion of legal/procurement contacts.
2. Offer to walk through SOW acceptance criteria.

## Day 12: Close Loop

1. If no response, send concise close-the-loop note.
2. Move to nurture with a defined re-engagement date.
